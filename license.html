Contract Compliance Notes:

  • HIGH RISK: Strong copyleft licenses that require disclosure of proprietary code and may create GPL obligations
  • MEDIUM RISK: Weak copyleft licenses that require disclosure but allow proprietary linking/combination
  • LOW RISK: Permissive licenses requiring only attribution, minimal compliance burden
  • Disclosure Required: Based on contract sections 16.7-16.9 requirements for "Included Open Source Software"
  • Family Licenses: GPL, LGPL, and CC families include multiple versions with similar characteristics
